Description:
	Demonstrates the -nofilelegends flag. File-defined legends are ignored; only user-defined legends from -dslegends are shown.
File(s) used:
	act_files/COULOMB-PC-elec.xvg act_files/COULOMB-PC+GS-elec.xvg
Command:
	plotxvg -f act_files/COULOMB-PC-elec.xvg act_files/COULOMB-PC+GS-elec.xvg -dslegends 'PC-elec' 'PC+GS-elec' -nofilelegends -eqax -sharelabel -panels side -save plotxvg_tests-output/10nofilelegends.pdf -noshow -quiet
