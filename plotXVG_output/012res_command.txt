Description:
Plots the residual of the data, meaning x is substracted from y for all data sets.
Statistics are based on original train and test values and will not be affected by the residual flag.
File(s) used:
act_xvgfiles/COULOMB.xvg act_xvgfiles/EPOT.xvg
Command:
python plotxvg -f act_xvgfiles/COULOMB.xvg act_xvgfiles/EPOT.xvg -stats -panels -res -save plotXVG_output/012res.pdf -noshow
